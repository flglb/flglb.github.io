# **Flaglab – Privacy Policy**

_Last updated: 22.11.2025_

## **Introduction**
Flaglab is an Android application designed for creating and customizing flags. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

## **Information We Collect**
Flaglab **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

The app:
- Does **not** require you to create an account  
- Does **not** collect personal information  
- Does **not** track your activity  
- Does **not** send any data to external servers  

All actions and creations within the app stay on your device.

## **In-App Purchases**
Flaglab uses **Google Play Billing** for optional in-app purchases.  
All payment processing and related data (such as purchase history or payment method) are handled **solely by Google Play**.  
Flaglab itself does **not** access, collect, or store any payment-related information.

For more details, please refer to Google’s privacy policies.

## **Third-Party Services**
The app does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or third-party tracking services.

The only third-party service involved is Google Play Billing, as described above.

## **Children’s Privacy**
Because Flaglab does not collect or transmit any data, it is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## **Your Rights**
Since Flaglab does not collect personal data:
- We do not store anything that could be accessed, modified, or deleted.
- There is no data for which you need to request access or removal.

## **Changes to This Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated “Last Updated” date.
